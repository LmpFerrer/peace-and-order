Barangay Teachers Camp

 Teacher's Camp is one of the most preserved places here in Baguio. Not only preserved, it is also well-mantained when it comes to cleanliness and the beauty of environment. It is one of the tourist's spots here in Baguio that's why many people especially students from other provinces and cities chooses to stay in Teacher's Camp which serves as their field trip.

 We created this simple website with the following reasons:

 Mission

To inform people, families and tourist living in the teachers camp about the rules and regulation implemented in this barangay where all of them are required to diligently follow and know.

 Vission

To have a peaceful community having a low rate of disorder and disagrement and where anyone can live happly and harmoniously.

Our Motto:

Rules & Regulations = Peace & Order



Rules and Regulation

 These are the set of rules implemented in the barangay where all people should know and follow inorder for us to have a peaceful community and barangay.

Curfew

 A curfer hour has been implemented for all the people living in this barangay. For children they should be at home before 7pm and for young adults or adults, 10pm.
 Parents or guardians of children found on streets and violating the statute for the third time would be required to pay between P500 and P1,000 or render community service from five to 10 days

Smoking Ban

 Last year the Ordinance No. 34 series of 2017 or the â€œSmoke-Free Baguio Ordinance" has been approved.

 Person who is caught smoking on the phrohibited areas will be fined P1,000 for first offense, P2,000 for second offense and P3,000 for third offense

Prohibited Areas

  • enclosed or partially enclosed areas
  • public conveyance
  • work place
  • unauthorized Designated Smoking Areas/Designated Vaping Areas
  • government vehicles
  • other public place

 Commiting prohibited acts such as Selling or distributing, purchasing, and ordering tobacco products/ and electronic nicotine delivery systems will be punishable with fines P2,000 for first offense, P3,000 for second and P5,000 for third offense.

Karaoke

 Filipinos are very fond of karaoke, everytime a filino celebrates an occation a karaoke machine will always be there.

 The problem of using the karaoke machine is that even if its pass midnight they are till using it, making there neighbors irritated by the loud noise they're making. Thats why the barangay implemented a noise reduction rule that they're just allowed to use the karaoke machine until 10pm, when they excede the time limit a barangay tanod will be warning them.

"Inuman"

 Passing to a sari-sari store and seeing group of people dringking alcohol is just a normal view before for filipinos but as time passes by its not a plesant view and usually its the cause of conflict and fighting of drunk people.

 To avoid this scenarios that will cause disturbance in barangay they prohibited the sari-sari store owners to allow people drinking near or around there store.

No Parking

 Traffic is one of the problems experienced by the citizens of Baguio City. To have an open road that a vehicle can make as an alternate road, barangay put a "No Parking" sign in certain places.

 Vehicle owners should follow this signs in this barangay inorder for other vehicle can use the following roads with ease.
